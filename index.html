Welcome to Tiny Swimming

At Tiny Swimming we believe that learning to swim should be fun, nurturing, gentle and engaging, and enable your child to become familiar with the new experience of swimming in a safe, warm environment.

All of our lessons focus on taking the child’s lead, developing water confidence and swimming technique by providing opportunities for sensory discovery in the pool.
